About Us

Yi Feng International is proud to have been serving Canada since 2009. We have taken great care in establishing our excellent reputation for outstanding customer service and unbeatable price. We offer various transportation services, such as airport transfers, packaged tours, private charter and etc. We pride ourselves on being able to offer comfortable ride at an affordable price. Whether you are traveling for pleasure or business, please sit back, relax and let Yi Feng Shuttle & Tours do the driving.

关于我们

逸风国际是一家注册于加拿大的国际旅游机构，公司成立于2009年。我们拥有出色的客户服务和无与伦比的价格优势，为您提供全方位的旅游/运输服务，如机场接送，旅行团，私人包机等。我们非常自豪地以最优惠的价格让您感受极致的旅游服务体验。不管您是商务出差或是休闲自助游，请坐下来，放轻松，让逸风做驾驶带您畅游北美。
